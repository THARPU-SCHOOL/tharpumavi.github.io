CGPA Calculation — NEB Method

  • Each subject carries equal credit weight (1 credit each)
  • For subjects with practical: Subject GPA = Theory GPA × 0.75 + Practical GPA × 0.25
  • For subjects without practical (e.g. Math, Economics): Subject GPA = Theory GPA only
  • CGPA = Sum of all Subject GPAs ÷ Total number of graded subjects
  • Minimum D grade (35%) required separately in both theory AND practical
  • Any component below 35% = NG (Not Graded) — student cannot pass that subject
  • Minimum CGPA of 1.6 (Grade D) required to pass; CGPA ≥ 2.0 recommended for Grade 12 Science
